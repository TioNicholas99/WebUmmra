Ihram - The Sacred State

Step 1

What is Ihram?

Ihram is the sacred state a Muslim must enter before performing Umrah or Hajj. It involves wearing specific garments and abstaining from certain actions.

Key Points:

  • Men wear two white unsewn sheets of cloth
  • Women wear regular modest clothing
  • Recitation of Talbiyah prayer
  • Entering the state of physical and spiritual purity

Practical Tips:

Purchase quality Ihram garments before your journey. Practice wearing them to ensure comfort. Bring a belt to secure the lower garment and extra sets in case of soiling.

Tawaf - Circumambulation of the Kaaba

Step 2

What is Tawaf?

Tawaf is the ritual of circumambulating (walking around) the Kaaba seven times in a counterclockwise direction. It symbolizes the unity of believers in worship of the One God.

Key Points:

  • Begin at the Black Stone (Hajar al-Aswad)
  • Complete seven circuits around the Kaaba
  • Make special prayers during each circuit
  • Men perform Ramal (brisk walking) during the first three rounds

Practical Tips:

Choose less crowded times for Tawaf if possible. Stay hydrated. Keep focused on your prayers rather than jostling for position at the Black Stone during crowded periods.
